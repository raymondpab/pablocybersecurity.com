About Us

Secure the Data you Value

Equipped with more than a decade of industry knowledge and expertise, CYBER SECURITY (A New Era). is a 100% Filipino-owned IT and cybersecurity solutions distributor that can help safeguard the bloodline of your growing company – your business data. We are comprised of certified experts and professionals in the field of data security, protection, and compliance. We take a strategic inside-out approach to provide you with sustainable and risk-ready systems that fortifies your entire infrastructure; not only today but also in the future.

As a value-added cybersecurity partner of SMEs in the country, we take a pragmatic approach to help you conquer your most pressing security issues in compliance with R.A 10173 or The Data Privacy Act of 2012. Our extensive hands-on experience in handling these concerns have allowed us to give you proper solutions which can future-proof your data protection strategy – from IT Infrastructure Security, Remote Access and Monitoring Security, and Email & Data Security.

We know that cybersecurity is a broad topic to cover. This is the reason why we have created FREE resources that can help minimize your learning curve on specific data security topics – ranging from types of malware, how to protect yourself from online threats, cybersecurity training for employees, and more.
